    6 Week Class

    If your dog has already mastered the basics of obedience training, advanced group classes can take their skills to the next level. These classes are designed for dogs who have completed a beginner obedience course and are ready to learn more complex commands and behaviors. Advanced obedience classes often focus on refining existing skills and introducing new commands. Your dog may learn more challenging exercises such as heeling off-leash, staying in place for extended periods, and responding to commands from a distance. These skills are invaluable for building a well-trained and reliable companion. Beyond the practical benefits, advanced obedience classes can also be a fun and rewarding experience for both you and your dog. The challenges and mental stimulation provided by these classes can help to keep your dog engaged and happy. Additionally, attending advanced classes can strengthen the bond between you and your dog as you work together to achieve new goals. Whether you're looking to compete in obedience trials or simply want to continue training your dog for personal enjoyment, advanced group obedience classes are a great way to take your dog's training to the next level.
